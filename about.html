Our Story

Building Futures Through Quality Education

Established in 2003, Active Commerce Centre has been a beacon of excellence in commerce education in Patna. What started as a small coaching center with a vision to provide quality education has now grown into one of the most trusted names in commerce coaching.

Over the years, we have successfully guided more than 10,000 students towards their academic and career goals. Our comprehensive approach combines traditional teaching methods with modern educational techniques to ensure every student receives personalized attention.

We specialize in 11th & 12th Commerce, CA Foundation, B.Com preparation, and various competitive exams. Our experienced faculty and proven teaching methodology have consistently delivered outstanding results.
